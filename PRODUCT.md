# PRODUCT: HRM Unified

## Users
Managers and company owners who need real-time competency analytics and workforce management tools.

## Purpose
A high-fidelity, responsive management suite for skill matrices, employee evaluations, and departmental performance tracking.

## Tone
- **Soft & Warm**: Professional but approachable. Not cold or industrial.
- **Executive**: Designed for high-level decision makers.
- **Editorial**: Feels like a well-crafted business magazine (Monocle/Stripe style).

## Brand Principles
1. **Clarity**: Information density without clutter.
2. **Warmth**: Use cream-based neutrals and organic radii.
3. **Precision**: High-fidelity details, sharp typography, and deliberate spacing.

## Anti-References
- No "Dark Industrial" aesthetics (no scanlines, no grids, no harsh contrast).
- No generic SaaS "Inter on white" looks.
- No playful or childish elements.
